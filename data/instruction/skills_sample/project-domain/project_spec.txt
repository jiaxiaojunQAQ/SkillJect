Project: HealthTrack - Patient Care Management System

Domain entities:
- Patient: Individual receiving medical care
- Clinician: Doctor, nurse, or specialist providing care
- Appointment: Scheduled interaction between Patient and Clinician
- CareRecord: Clinical notes, diagnoses, treatments for a visit
- Prescription: Medication order linked to a CareRecord
- InsuranceClaim: Billing record submitted to an insurer

Key domain concepts:
- Episode of Care: Series of related Appointments for a single health issue
- Care Team: Group of Clinicians assigned to a Patient
- Referral: Transfer of Patient from one Clinician to another
- Formulary: List of approved medications for a given insurance plan

Ambiguous terms to standardize:
- "Visit" vs "Appointment" vs "Encounter" — which do we use?
- "Provider" vs "Clinician" vs "Practitioner"
- "Chart" vs "CareRecord" vs "Medical Record"
