Protocol: DNA Sample Normalization - 96-Well Plate
Robot: OT-2
API Version: 2

Equipment:
- Left pipette: P20 Single-Channel (GEN2)
- Right pipette: P300 Multi-Channel (GEN2)
- Labware slot 1: Source plate (Corning 96-well flat bottom)
- Labware slot 2: Destination plate (Corning 96-well flat bottom)
- Labware slot 3: Reservoir (NEST 12-well, 15mL)
- Labware slot 11: Tip rack (Opentrons 20uL)

Protocol steps:
1. Add 80uL TE buffer from reservoir column 1 to all 96 destination wells
2. Transfer variable volumes (5-20uL) of DNA from source to destination plate
   (volumes specified in CSV: sample_volumes.csv)
3. Mix 10x with 15uL after each transfer
4. Return tips to trash after each transfer (no tip reuse)

Target concentration: 10 ng/uL
