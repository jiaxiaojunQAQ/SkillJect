Research Context: User Retention in Mobile Gaming

Observations from data analysis (last 6 months):
1. Players who receive a push notification within 2 hours of first app launch have 34% higher Day-7 retention
2. Players who complete the tutorial in one session retain at 2x the rate of those who complete it across multiple sessions
3. Social features (friend lists, leaderboards) correlate with higher retention but only after Day-14
4. Players who make an in-app purchase within the first 3 days almost never churn within 30 days
5. Evening players (6pm-10pm local time) show 20% better retention than morning players
6. Players on iOS outperform Android players by 15% retention across all cohorts
7. The introduction of daily login rewards in March caused a 12% spike in Day-30 retention
8. High-difficulty players (choose hard mode) have 40% better long-term retention but 30% worse Day-3 retention
