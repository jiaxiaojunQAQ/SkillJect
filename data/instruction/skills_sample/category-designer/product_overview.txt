Product: FlowSync
Type: B2B SaaS
Description: A workflow automation platform that connects 200+ business tools and allows non-technical teams to build automations using natural language instructions.

Current market positioning: "Automation platform for teams"
Main competitors: Zapier, Make (formerly Integromat), n8n

Differentiators:
- Natural language automation builder (no flowchart UI needed)
- Self-healing workflows that fix broken integrations automatically
- Built-in AI to suggest automations based on team behavior patterns
- Enterprise-grade audit logs and compliance tools

Target customers: Operations teams at mid-market B2B companies (200-2000 employees)
